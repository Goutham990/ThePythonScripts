Current Account with Transaction Fees: You can receive all the payments on your current bank account, remembering that the bank charges you for each payment i,

New Special Account with Upfront Fee: You can pay the bank d dollars upfront to open a new special account for which the bank doesn't charge you for any of the upcoming transactions.

Your task is to decide which of the above two options is more profitable for you. If both ways are equally profitable, then you prefer to be charged for each transaction.

Input Format

The first line contains a single integer q denoting the number of scenarios to handle. After that, descriptions of all these scenarios follow. In the first line of a single scenario, there are 4 space-separated integers n, k, x, d. In the second line, there are n space-separated integers p_0,p_1,
ldots,p_n−1.
